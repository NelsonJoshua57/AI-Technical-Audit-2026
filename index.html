05 — Key insights

01
Gemini zero-citation is the most urgent issue. Google's own AI platform returning no citations for Winkworth indicates fundamental indexing or E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust) gaps that will also suppress Google AI Overview performance.
02
Foxtons' dominance across all platforms (1,238 total) suggests strong structured data and authoritative content. Foxtons' Google AIO lead (284 vs 46) is likely driven by robust schema markup, FAQ content, and backlink authority — all directly replicable.
03
Perplexity gap is the largest in absolute terms (12 vs 303). Perplexity indexes real-time web content — this suggests Winkworth's content is either not being discovered or not considered authoritative enough to surface in response to estate agent queries.
04
Grok is Winkworth's relative strength. With 64 citations (vs Foxtons 350), Winkworth performs best proportionally on Grok. If Winkworth has an active X/Twitter presence, this can be leveraged as a GEO quick win.

Deploy structured data across all key pages

P1 — Immediate
